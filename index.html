Honghong HE

I am HE Honghong (贺红红), a Ph.D. student at Hong Kong Polytechnic University, supervised by Prof. Tracy Mok. My current research focuses on generative models and their applications in the fashion domain. I also have some exploration of 3d model reconstruction, AR, and VR, kindly scroll down to view my design works.
